ABOUT US

Innovating Healthcare Through Sustainable Technology

Our Journey & Scale

A.M DIGITAL HEALTH HUB was established as a registered, forward-thinking enterprise dedicated to transforming how communities and healthcare organizations access and utilize digital systems.

Operating at the intersection of technology, health informatics, and capacity building, we have rapidly scaled our impact from local rural interventions to a massive cross-border operation. Today, our operational footprint spans across Nigeria with international reach extending into the Republic of Niger, Chad, and Cameroon.

We provide a comprehensive suite of services, from deploying localized digital EMR systems to executing vast capacity-building programs, ensuring that underserved regions are fully equipped to sustain the future of healthcare.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower underserved communities and institutions through accessible, innovative IT solutions. We aim to enhance healthcare equity, eliminate medical record fragmentation, and promote evidence-based, sustainable decision-making.

👁️ Our Vision

To become Africa’s foremost hub for digital health transformation—recognized globally for operational excellence, grassroots technological innovation, and measurable, long-term social impact.

🛡️

Data Security & Compliance

Handling over 2 million patient records requires uncompromising trust. We operate with robust security protocols, adhering strictly to national data protection regulations (such as NDPR) and global health data standards. Every digital EMR system we deploy ensures that patient records are encrypted, securely anonymized for public health reporting, and safely backed up against infrastructural failures.

Advancing Global Goals

Our cross-border operations are deeply aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), ensuring that our local technological solutions contribute directly to global progress.

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being

By digitizing patient records and implementing resilient EMR systems, we drastically improve healthcare access, operational efficiency, and long-term patient outcomes across underserved regions.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Through our continuous capacity-building programs, we empower thousands of local health workers, students, and youths with critical digital literacy and sustainable IT skills.

SDG 9: Industry & Innovation

We build and deploy localized, sustainable technological infrastructure that bridges the digital divide, ensuring rural and off-grid healthcare facilities are fully modernized.

Our Leadership

Hassan Al-Mujtaba Ja'afar

Founder, CEO & Managing Director

Hassan Al-Mujtaba Ja'afar is a highly driven social entrepreneur and IT professional with specialized training in Health Information Management. Recognizing that technology is the ultimate equalizer in global health, he established A.M Digital Health Hub, leading a dedicated team of professionals in revolutionizing digital systems across rural and urban centers.

Under his leadership, the Hub has rapidly scaled from local interventions to cross-border operations. Hassan is deeply committed to environmental sustainability and circular economy models, ensuring that the organization's technological deployments actively reduce medical waste and promote long-term resilience.

"I founded A.M Digital Health Hub with a singular mission: to ensure no community is left behind in the digital health revolution. We are here to serve, to train, and to build sustainable systems that outlast us."
